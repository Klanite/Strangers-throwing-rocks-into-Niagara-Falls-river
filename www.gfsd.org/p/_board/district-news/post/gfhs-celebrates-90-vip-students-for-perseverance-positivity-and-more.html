GFHS celebrates 84 "VIP" students for perseverance, positivity, and more!
Dec 13 2024

Eighty-four Glens Falls High School students were recognized this semester with a VIP award in one of the following areas:

  • Positivity (optimism, the student views challenges as opportunities for growth)
  • Kindness (the student is friendly and generous, the student builds others up)
  • Perseverance (persistence, the student doesn’t give up easily or may be making hard-fought improvements)
  • Love of Learning (the student is curious and has a thirst for deeper understanding)
  • GF Nation Spirit (the student demonstrates leadership and respect, donates time and energy to the community)

Each student who received a nomination was given a certificate and given the opportunity to participate in a make your own ice cream sundae event. Special thanks to Stewart's Shops for their donation of the ice cream and toppings for this celebration!  Please scroll down to read about each of the VIP award-winners’ recognition.
