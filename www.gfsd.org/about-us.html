About Us

Glens Falls City Schools provide UPK-12 public education for approximately 2,000 students living in the City of Glens Falls and part of the town of Queensbury within the Adirondack region of upstate New York. The city is located north of Saratoga Springs, on the banks of the Hudson River.

Our small-city district covers approximately four square miles, and students walk to and from one of three neighborhood elementary schools, or the middle and high school, which are situated next-door to each other on Quade Street. Glens Falls Common, a small K-6 district in the city, tuitions its grade 7-12 students into Glens Falls City Schools. They graduate as part of the Glens Falls High School senior class.

Learn more about our community and our schools in our District Profile
