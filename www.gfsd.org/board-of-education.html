MEETING SCHEDULE

The regular monthly meetings of the Board of Education are the second Monday of the month at 7 p.m. at the district office (15 Quade Street), unless school is not in session. In that case, the meeting dates move to the week before or the following Monday. The meeting schedule for 2024-25 is as follows:

Monday, July 15, 2024 (6:00 p.m.) Reorganizational Meeting, with regular meeting to follow at 6:15 p.m.
Monday, August 19, 2024 (6:00 p.m.)
Monday, September 9, 2024
Monday, October 21, 2024
Monday, November 18, 2024
Tuesday, December 17, 2024

Monday, February 10, 2025
Monday, March 10, 2025
Monday, April 7, 2025
Monday, May 12, 2025
